Fractal Dimension

What is Fractal Dimension?

Fractal dimension is a measure that describes how complex a shape is. Unlike normal dimensions (1D, 2D, 3D), fractals can have non-integer dimensions, showing how detail in a pattern changes with scale. It helps us understand structures that are too irregular for traditional geometry.

Why it is useful?

Fractal dimension reveals the hidden complexity of patterns that look chaotic at first glance. It is used in fields like image processing, nature analysis, medical imaging, and computer graphics to better understand patterns and structure.
